About the project

Renewable energy allows us endless access to energy. Unfortunately we have to harvest the energy when it is available.
EMDO is an energy manager that helps you to automate major loads. Major loads include things such as heat pumps, water heaters, laundry dryers and electric car. This increases the self-consumption quote whilst reducing the power consumption from the grid in order to save you money!
EMDO is a programmable energy manager and makes it easy to interface between different manufacturers. EMDO is easy programmable through its webinterface with BASIC programming language - an easy to learn but powerfull programming language.
